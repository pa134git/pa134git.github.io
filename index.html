PTA Activities

Here are some of the things we do as PTA:

  • Plan and run the Holiday Gift Shop in December
  • Organize the annual Picture Day
  • Fund the fifth grade yearbook, T-shirts and graduation party
  • Fund end-of-year celebration for Kindergarten
  • Show appreciation to the hard-working school staff (Photos of Staff Appreciation Day lunch - June 2024)
  • Survey the school community and work with the administration to enhance our children's experience through afterschool programs and enrichment activities
  • Apply for grants and reach out to local government officials to raise money for large projects such as the schoolyard renovations

COMING UP:

Meetings

Schedule & Minutes

The PTA meets once a month to discuss school business and plan activities. All parents are welcome to attend. Please do not forget to bring ID to enter the school building.

In the 2024-2025 school year, we plan to alternate between in-person meetings at the school and Zoom-only meetings in the evening. Stay tuned for more details!

How can you contribute to the PTA?

Here are some ways you can help make a difference

  • Attend our monthly meetings: Schedule & Minutes
  • Sign up to our mailing list
  • Volunteer some of your time to help with the Holiday Shop or other events
  • Email the PTA board any ideas you have for improving our school and community to 22k134pa@schools.nyc.gov
  • Donate to help fund PTA endeavors by using our Zelle and Venmo accounts
